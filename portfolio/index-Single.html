From Designing the Physical World to Exploring the Digital World: A Lifelong Learner

With a background in architecture, I developed strong problem-solving, analytical, and technical skills essential for user-centered design. Tools like AutoCAD, 3Ds Max, and Photoshop sharpened my attention to detail and visualization.

As a research assistant, I apply my expertise in design, visualization, spatial thinking, and technical skills in Python, Excel, and C# to develop systems, applications, and analyze data. Collaborating with diverse teams has broadened my approach to problem-solving.

I enjoy coding to create digital solutions, user-friendly applications, and tackle real-world challenges. This journey led me to a web development bootcamp and Harvard's "CS50 Web Programming with Python and JavaScript" course. I'm excited to continue learning and growing in tech and applying my unique blend of design and technical skills to create impactful solutions.
